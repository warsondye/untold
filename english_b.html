2020 WASSCE English Section B Questions & Answers

English Language

Question 1: Write a letter to your principal discussing the importance of discipline in the school.

Answer: The letter should introduce the topic, outline key reasons why discipline is important, provide examples, and conclude with a polite appeal.

Question 2: Describe a memorable day in your life.

Answer: The response should include a clear description of the event, emotions felt, and why it was memorable.

Question 3: Explain how social media influences students' academic performance.

Answer: The essay should discuss positive and negative impacts, including distraction, research benefits, and communication.

Question 4: Write a speech on "The Role of Youth in National Development."

Answer: The speech should highlight contributions of youth in technology, leadership, and community service.

Question 5: Discuss the effects of climate change on agriculture in your country.

Answer: The essay should explain rising temperatures, unpredictable rainfall, and their impact on crop production and food security.
